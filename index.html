My name is Derek Alexander Smith, an indie songwriter and musician from Brooklyn, NY. The music I make typically hovers in the crossroads of rock, folk, and country - but with a lot of varied influences. Some of my favorite artists are Sturgill Simpson, The Grateful Dead, Peter Tosh, Oliver Mtukudzi, Milton Nascimento, Madison Cunningham, Joey Landreth and Joni Mitchell.
